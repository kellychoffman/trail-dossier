▲ How it stacks up against your training

The climbiest-per-mile day you ride.

At 211 ft of climbing per mile, Sturdy Dirty packs in more climbing per mile than your typical Tiger lap (~185 ft/mi) or even Dirty Bloom (~170 ft/mi) — about 40% of Dirty Bloom's total climbing in a third of the distance, so roughly 2 Tiger laps back-to-back but punchier. Two sustained 1,000–1,500 ft climbs (to mile ~5.8 and ~11.4), with pitches hitting ~14%. Train it: climb-dense laps + Z5 efforts on the steep stuff, and dial in fueling for a ~2.5–3.5 hr push.
